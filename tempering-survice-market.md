<p><p>Tempering service market encompasses three main types based on temperature ranges. Low-Temperature Tempering typically occurs between 300°F and 500°F, enhancing toughness while minimizing brittleness. Medium-Temperature Tempering, around 500°F to 900°F, balances strength and ductility, ideal for tools and structural components. High-Temperature Tempering, above 900°F, improves hardness and impact resistance, often used in high-stress applications. Each type caters to specific material properties, ensuring optimized performance for various industries, including automotive, aerospace, and manufacturing.</p></p>
