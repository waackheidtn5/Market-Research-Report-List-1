<p><p>Reinsurance services are categorized into two primary types: Property and Casualty (P&C) Reinsurance and Life Reinsurance. P&C Reinsurance involves coverage for risks associated with property damage and liability claims, helping insurers manage large losses from events like natural disasters. Life Reinsurance, on the other hand, focuses on risks related to life insurance policies, providing support to insurers in managing mortality risk and ensuring financial stability. Both types play a crucial role in enhancing the resilience of insurance companies.</p></p>
