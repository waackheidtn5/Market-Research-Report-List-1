<p><strong>The Headless Compression Screws Market Analysis by types is segmented into:</strong></p>
<p><ul><li>Fully Threaded Screw</li><li>Both Ends Threaded Screw</li></ul></p>
<p><p>The headless compression screws market is categorized into fully threaded screws and both ends threaded screws. Fully threaded screws feature a consistent thread pattern along their entire length, providing enhanced grip and stability in various applications. Conversely, both ends threaded screws have threads on both ends but remain unthreaded in the middle section, allowing for effective compression while accommodating specific design requirements. Both types are essential in orthopedic and dental procedures, offering precise fixation without the intrusion of a head.</p></p>
