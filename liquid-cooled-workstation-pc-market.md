<p><p>The liquid cooled workstation PC market features two primary types: All-in-One (AIO) liquid cooling and Custom Loop liquid cooling. AIO systems integrate the cooling components into a single unit, simplifying installation and maintenance while offering efficient cooling for high-performance workstations. In contrast, Custom Loop liquid cooling allows for greater customization and flexibility, enabling users to design a cooling system tailored to their specific hardware needs. Both options enhance thermal management, contributing to improved performance and longevity of workstation components.</p></p>
