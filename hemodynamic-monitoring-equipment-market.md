<p><p>The hemodynamic monitoring equipment market is categorized into three main types: </p><p>1. **Noninvasive Hemodynamic Monitoring Devices** measure cardiovascular parameters without penetrating the body, using methods like oscillometry or bioimpedance. </p><p>   </p><p>2. **Minimally Invasive Hemodynamic Monitoring Devices** involve minor procedures, such as placing catheters in peripheral vessels to acquire real-time data with reduced risk. </p><p>3. **Invasive Hemodynamic Monitoring Devices** require significant surgical procedures, like arterial or central venous catheterization, providing precise and comprehensive cardiovascular assessments in critically ill patients.</p></p>
