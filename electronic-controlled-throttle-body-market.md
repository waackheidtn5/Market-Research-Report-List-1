<p><p>The Electronic Controlled Throttle Body market comprises three main types: actuators, throttle plates, and throttle position sensors. Actuators control the throttle's opening and closing, ensuring precise air intake regulation. Throttle plates physically manage airflow into the engine, contributing to performance and efficiency. Throttle position sensors monitor the position of the throttle plate, providing real-time data to the engine control unit for optimal engine response. Together, these components enhance engine performance, fuel efficiency, and emissions control in modern vehicles.</p></p>
