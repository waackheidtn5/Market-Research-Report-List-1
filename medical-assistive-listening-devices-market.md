<p><p>The Medical Assistive Listening Devices market includes various types of auditory aids designed to enhance hearing. Ear-based hearing aids are worn in or behind the ear, amplifying sounds for individuals with hearing loss. Bone-anchored hearing aids utilize bone conduction to transmit sound directly to the inner ear, suitable for those with specific types of hearing impairment. Cochlear implants are surgically implanted devices that convert sound into electrical signals, providing auditory perception for individuals with severe to profound hearing loss.</p></p>
