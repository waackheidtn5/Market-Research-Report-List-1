<p><p>The Spark Erosion Oil market is categorized into two main types based on viscosity: low viscosity and high viscosity spark erosion oils. Low viscosity spark erosion oils are fluid, enabling better flow and heat dissipation during the erosion process, making them suitable for intricate precision tasks. In contrast, high viscosity spark erosion oils possess thicker consistency, providing enhanced lubrication and stability, which is beneficial for high-temperature applications and prolonged machining operations. Both types cater to diverse industrial needs in electrical discharge machining.</p></p>
