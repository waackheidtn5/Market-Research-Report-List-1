<p><ul><li>Barometer</li><li>Anemometer</li><li>Hygrometer</li><li>Rain Gauge</li><li>Thermometer</li><li>Sling Psychrometer</li><li>Weather Ballons</li></ul></p>
<p><p>Weather forecasting systems encompass various market types including barometers, anemometers, hygrometers, rain gauges, thermometers, sling psychrometers, and weather balloons. Barometers measure atmospheric pressure, aiding in storm prediction. Anemometers gauge wind speed, crucial for weather patterns. Hygrometers assess humidity levels. Rain gauges quantify precipitation, while thermometers record temperature changes. Sling psychrometers measure humidity using wet and dry bulb temperatures. Weather balloons capture upper-atmosphere data, enhancing forecasting accuracy. Together, these instruments provide essential data for accurate weather predictions.</p></p>
