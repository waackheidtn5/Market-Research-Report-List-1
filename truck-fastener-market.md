<p><strong>The Truck Fastener Market Analysis by types is segmented into:</strong></p>
<p><ul><li>Threaded Fasteners</li><li>Non-Threaded Fasteners</li></ul></p>
<p><p>The truck fastener market is divided into two main types: threaded and non-threaded fasteners. Threaded fasteners, such as bolts and screws, feature helical ridges that allow for secure assembly and disassembly, making them ideal for various truck components. Non-threaded fasteners include items like rivets and clips, which provide a permanent joint without threads and are often used in applications where vibration resistance is crucial. Both types play essential roles in ensuring the structural integrity and safety of trucks.</p></p>
