<p><strong>The Ethernet over Coax (EoC) Equipment Market Analysis by types is segmented into:</strong></p>
<p><ul><li>Passive EOC</li><li>Active EOC</li></ul></p>
<p><p>The Ethernet over Coax (EoC) equipment market can be categorized into two main types: passive EoC and active EoC. Passive EoC utilizes existing coaxial cables without amplification, providing a cost-effective solution for extending network connections. It is generally simpler to deploy but may offer limited range and performance. In contrast, active EoC incorporates signal amplifiers and repeaters to enhance performance and extend connectivity over longer distances, making it suitable for more demanding applications requiring higher bandwidth and reliability.</p></p>
