<p><p>The consumer-grade inkjet printer market primarily features two technologies: piezoelectric and thermal types. Piezoelectric printers use a crystal mechanism to control ink droplet ejection, offering precision and versatility, making them ideal for photo printing. In contrast, thermal printers heat ink to create droplets, which is simpler and generally more cost-effective, suitable for everyday document printing. Both types cater to different user needs, driving market segmentation based on performance preferences, printing quality, and operational costs.</p></p>
