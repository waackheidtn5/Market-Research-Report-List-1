<p><strong>The Single Use Cystoscope Market Analysis by types is segmented into:</strong></p>
<p><ul><li>Flexible Single Use Cystoscopes</li><li>Rigid Single Use Cystoscopes</li></ul></p>
<p><p>The single-use cystoscope market is divided into two main types: flexible and rigid single-use cystoscopes. Flexible single-use cystoscopes are designed to navigate easily through the urinary tract, providing enhanced maneuverability and visualization during procedures. They are often preferred for diagnostic and therapeutic interventions. Rigid single-use cystoscopes, on the other hand, feature a fixed design that offers stability and precision for surgical operations. Both types aim to reduce the risk of infection and eliminate the need for sterilization between uses.</p></p>
