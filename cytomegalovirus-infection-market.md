<p><p>Cytomegalovirus (CMV) infection treatment includes several antiviral agents, each serving specific roles. Cidofovir, administered intravenously, is an alternative for resistant strains and severe cases. Foscarnet, another option, is effective against resistant CMV but has significant renal side effects. These agents collectively address CMV-related diseases, with market dynamics influenced by efficacy, safety profiles, and resistance patterns.</p></p>
