<p><p>Veterinary dental handpieces are categorized into three main types based on their power sources. Electric-powered handpieces offer consistent torque and speed, enabling precise dental procedures easily. Air-powered handpieces rely on compressed air, providing lightweight options and high-speed cutting capabilities. Ultrasonic-powered handpieces utilize ultrasonic vibrations for effective plaque removal and cleaning, making them ideal for periodontal treatments. Each type serves unique functions in veterinary dentistry, catering to different procedural requirements and enhancing oral care for animals.</p></p>
