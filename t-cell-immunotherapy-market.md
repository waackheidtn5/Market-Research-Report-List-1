<p><p>The T-Cell Immunotherapy market includes three primary types: CAR-T (Chimeric Antigen Receptor T-Cell) therapy, TCR (T-Cell Receptor) therapy, and TIL (Tumor-Infiltrating Lymphocyte) therapy. CAR-T therapy involves engineering T-cells to target specific cancer antigens, while TCR therapy enhances T-cells to recognize and attack tumor cells more effectively. TIL therapy uses T-cells harvested from tumors to activate and expand them in the lab before reinfusion. Each approach aims to harness the immune system to combat cancer more effectively.</p></p>
