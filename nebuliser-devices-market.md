<p><ul><li>Pneumatic Nebuliser</li><li>Ultrasonic Nebuliser</li><li>Mesh Nebuliser</li></ul></p>
<p><p>The nebulizer devices market comprises three main types: pneumatic, ultrasonic, and mesh nebulizers. Pneumatic nebulizers utilize compressed air to convert liquid medication into aerosols, making them widely used and effective. Ultrasonic nebulizers employ ultrasonic vibrations to produce fine mist and are quieter and more portable but may not be suitable for certain medications. Mesh nebulizers use a mesh membrane to create aerosolized medication, offering a compact design with efficient delivery and a shorter treatment time, ideal for patients needing on-the-go solutions.</p></p>
