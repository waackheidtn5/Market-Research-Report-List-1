<p><p>The paper plastic lamination adhesives market is categorized into three main types: solvent-based adhesives, solvent-free adhesives, and water-based adhesives. Solvent-free adhesives are environmentally friendly, providing effective adhesion without volatile organic compounds, appealing to eco-conscious consumers.
