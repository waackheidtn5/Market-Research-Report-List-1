Medium viscosity sealants are versatile, suitable for various materials, providing effective sealing with ease of application. High viscosity sealants offer enhanced gap-filling properties, ideal for uneven surfaces and high-stress environments. Each type serves distinct needs, ensuring effective sealing across diverse applications and environments.</p></p>
