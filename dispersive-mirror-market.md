<p><strong>The Dispersive Mirror Market Analysis by types is segmented into:</strong></p>
<p><ul><li>Gires-Tournois Interferometer (GTI) Mirror</li><li>Chirped Mirror</li></ul></p>
<p><p>The dispersive mirror market comprises specialized optical components designed to manipulate light through dispersion. Gires-Tournois Interferometer (GTI) mirrors employ multiple thin-film layers to create precise phase shifts, enabling high reflectivity over a specific wavelength range and enhancing performance in applications like solid-state lasers. Chirped mirrors, on the other hand, are engineered to compensate for dispersion across a broad spectrum, making them essential in ultrafast laser systems. Together, these mirrors play crucial roles in advancing photonics and laser technology.</p></p>
